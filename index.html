About
I am soon to be a Machine Learning DPhil student at St Peter's College, Oxford. I will be joining the Machine Learning Research Group and will be advised by Stephen Roberts.

I am excited by the potential of blackbox optimisation for reinforcement learning, and currently working on ways to make it more efficient by exploiting structure and diversity. I am also interested in compact neural networks, specifically for RL policies. Downstream, I am most interested in robotics and electronic trading.

I am immensely privileged to have some fantastic collaborators, namely Krzysztof Choromanski, Aldo Pacchiano and Yunhao Tang.
