About
I am soon to be a Machine Learning DPhil student at the University of Oxford. I will be joining the Machine Learning Research Group and will be advised by Stephen Roberts.

I believe blackbox optimisation has a part to play in reinforcement learning, and I am currently working on ways to explooit structure to make it more efficient. Downstream, I am most interested in robotics and electronic trading.

I am immensely privileged to have some fantastic collaborators, namely Krzysztof Choromanski, Aldo Pacchiano and Yunhao Tang.
